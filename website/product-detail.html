Working Principle

The Planetary Ball Mill operates on the principle of planetary motion, where the grinding jars rotate around their own axes while simultaneously revolving around a central axis. This dual motion creates a high centrifugal force that significantly enhances the grinding efficiency.

When the mill is in operation, the grinding balls inside the jars are subjected to both rotational and centrifugal forces, causing them to impact the material being ground with high energy. This results in efficient pulverization and homogenization of the sample.

The speed ratio between the revolution and rotation of the jars can be adjusted to optimize the grinding process for different materials and desired particle sizes.
