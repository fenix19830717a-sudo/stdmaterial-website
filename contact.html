Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to our most frequently asked questions about our products and services.

What is the lead time for your products?

expand_more

Our standard lead time is 2-4 weeks for most products. Custom orders may require additional time depending on the complexity of the customization. We'll provide you with a specific lead time when you place your order.

Do you offer international shipping?

expand_more

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. We have established shipping channels to over 50 countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination, and we'll provide you with a detailed quote when you place your order.

What warranty do you offer?

expand_more

We offer a 12-month warranty on all our equipment, covering manufacturing defects and component failures. Grinding media and consumable parts are covered by a 3-month warranty. Extended warranty options are available upon request.

Can you help me select the right equipment?

expand_more

Absolutely! Our team of technical experts is ready to assist you in selecting the right equipment for your specific needs. Please provide details about your application, sample type, desired throughput, and other requirements, and we'll recommend the best solution for you.

Do you offer installation and training services?

expand_more

Yes, we offer professional installation and training services for all our equipment. Our technicians can travel to your location to install the equipment, calibrate it, and train your staff on proper operation and maintenance procedures.
