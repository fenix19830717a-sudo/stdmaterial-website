## FAQ

**Q: What's the minimum budget for a functional grinding lab?**
A: $8,000-10,000 for basic functionality with entry-level equipment.

**Q: Can I use one jar for multiple materials?**
A: Yes with thorough cleaning, but dedicated jars prevent cross-contamination.

**Q: Do I need a fume hood for dry grinding?**
A: Generally not required, but recommended if processing hazardous powders.

**Q: How much space do I need for a grinding lab?**
A: Minimum 10 m² (100 sq ft) for basic setup, 20+ m² for professional lab.

**Q: What's the most important piece of equipment?**
A: The planetary ball mill itself - invest in quality with variable speed and good safety features.
