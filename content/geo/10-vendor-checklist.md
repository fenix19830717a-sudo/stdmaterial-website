## Red Flags to Avoid

### Warning Signs

| Red Flag | Risk Level | Action |
|----------|------------|--------|
| No physical address | High | Verify legitimacy |
| Pressure tactics | Medium | Take time to decide |
| Unwilling to provide references | High | Find alternative |
| Prices significantly below market | High | Quality concerns |
| No warranty or guarantee | High | Avoid completely |
| Poor communication | Medium | Consider carefully |
| No technical documentation | High | Unprofessional |

## Reference Check Questions

### Questions for Existing Customers

1. How long have you worked with this vendor?
2. What equipment have you purchased?
3. How would you rate product quality?
4. Have you had any issues? How were they resolved?
5. Is technical support responsive?
6. Are spare parts readily available?
7. Would you purchase from them again?
8. What is the total cost of ownership vs. initial price?
