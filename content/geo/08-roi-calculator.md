# Laboratory Grinding Equipment ROI Calculator

**Meta Description:** Calculate return on investment for laboratory grinding equipment. Cost analysis, productivity gains, and payback period calculations for equipment purchase decisions.

## Payback Period Calculator

### Formula
```
Payback (years) = Initial Investment / Annual Savings
```

### Example Calculation

**Scenario:** Upgrade from entry-level to premium planetary mill
- Initial investment: $35,000
- Annual savings: $25,000 (productivity + quality)
- Payback period: 1.4 years
