Our Story

Founded in 2009, Hunan Shengtongda Materials Technology Co., Ltd. has grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer of industrial grinding equipment. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us a trusted partner for companies worldwide.

We specialize in planetary ball mills, grinding media, and accessories for laboratories and industrial production. Our products are used in materials science, pharmaceuticals, metallurgy, ceramics, and many other industries.
